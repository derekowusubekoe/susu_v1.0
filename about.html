Mutual Susu

Susu is a term used in Africa specifically Ghana meaning “little savings”. The African continent has grown to love such means of saving and understood it perfectly, but there is some challenges with this great way of saving.

The underlining features of this app are very simple to user friendly and they include;

  • CREATE ACCOUNT/LOGIN
  • DEPOSIT MOINEY
  • TRANSFER MONEY
  • INVESTMENT
  • ENQUIRES (Susu Balance, Mini Statement, Statement Request)
  • LOANS
  • UTILITIES (Water, Power, Cable Recharge, Credit/Data Recharge)
  • CUSTOMER CARE

The long run result will cut down cost and reduce the human interferences and embezzlement of monies. It will also build trust and assurance for the company and the end users.
